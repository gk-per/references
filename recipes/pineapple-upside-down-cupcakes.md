# Pineapple Upside Down Cake
## Ingredients
### Topping
- 1/4 cup melted butter
- 1/2 cup dark brown sugar
- Pineapple slices
- Jar cherries

### Cake
- 1.5 cups cake flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup butter (room temp)
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 2 large egg whites (room temp)
- 1/3 cup sour cream
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1/4 cup pineapple juice
- 2 tbsp milk (room temp)


## Steps
1) Preheat oven to 350
2) Prepare topping by pouring melted butter into a pie dish, sprinkling with brown sugar, and arranging fruit. Put in fridge.
3) Whisk cake flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together
4) Cream butter (1-2 mins) with mixer then beat in egg whites until combined
5) Beat in sour cream and vanilla extract
6) Pour dry ingredients into wet
7) On a low speed, mix ingredients and add milk and pineapple juice
8) Remove topping from fridge. Spread cake batter over topping.
9) Bake for 40 mins, tenting foil halfway through 
10) Remove cake from oven and cool on a wire rack before inverting
